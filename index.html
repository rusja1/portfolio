About

Lana
Ihoshyna

Ruslana Ihoshyna. Final-year Creative Digital Media student at MTU Cork and Design Student Leader in the Media Communications department.

I work across UI/UX, interactive design, front-end development, motion graphics, 3D modelling, and print design. I'm comfortable moving between creative and technical work, from building and deploying web platforms to animating in After Effects, modelling in Blender, designing for print, and everything in between. I approach projects with attention to both how things look and how they work.

I've worked in educational technology, science communication, and institutional design, with experience delivering real-world projects for clients including Teagasc and Crawford College of Art and Design.
