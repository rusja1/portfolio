Originally from Ukraine, now based in Fermoy, Cork. I'm a final-year Creative Digital Media student at MTU with a background in visual art, multimedia production, and science communication. My work spans interactive design, UI/UX, and creative technology — always with an eye on making things that actually work for real people.

I've worked as Design Student Leader in MTU's Media Communications department, completed a multimedia internship at Teagasc, and most recently built Cook&Learn — a hybrid physical-digital maths kit that won Best Prototype at the MTU Prize for Innovation.
