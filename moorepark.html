Moorepark Open Day Map

A visitor map created from scratch for the Teagasc Moorepark Open Day. The map guided attendees on the day and was also published in the Farmers Journal ahead of the event.

Year 2025
Client Teagasc
Published Farmers Journal
Tools Adobe Illustrator · Adobe After Effects · Canva
1 / 3

The Brief

The Moorepark Open Day is a large public event at Teagasc's research facility. I was tasked with creating a visitor map from scratch, gathering and translating site information from multiple departments into a clear, accessible layout that attendees could follow on the day.

The map was also published in the Farmers Journal ahead of the event, reaching a wider audience before the day itself.

My Process

I managed the project independently while coordinating communication across teams and print vendors simultaneously. All site information was gathered directly from departments and translated into the final design. I also produced a short motion version using After Effects.
